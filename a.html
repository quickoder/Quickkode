Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical kitchen or bathroom remodel take?

Most bathroom remodels take between 2 to 4 weeks, while full kitchen remodels usually range between 4 to 7 weeks depending on scope and custom material delivery.

Do I need to pull city permits myself?

No! We handle the entire plan submission, permitting, and inspection schedule with your local city department completely in-house.

Are you licensed and insured in California?

Yes, we are fully licensed, bonded, and insured for general contracting in the state of California.

Can I stay in my home during construction?

Yes, for single-room remodels (like a kitchen or master bathroom), most homeowners choose to stay in their home. We isolate construction zones with containment walls to minimize dust and noise.
