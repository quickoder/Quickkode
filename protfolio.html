Why is there a $20 starting fee?

The $20 initial payment secures your spot in our development queue and covers your intake review. The entire $20 is credited toward your final setup balance.

What happens during the 15-minute consultation?

We briefly talk through your intake responses, confirm your domain details, answer any questions, and ensure we have everything required before coding.

Are there long-term contracts?

No long-term lock-ins. Monthly hosting and maintenance fees are billed month-to-month, and you can cancel anytime.

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely. As your business grows, you can seamlessly add pages, custom forms, or advanced features by upgrading your tier.
